# AT&T Fiber Ad Campaign - Creative Brief
## February 2025 Multi-Offer Campaign

---

## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

**Campaign Name:** "Fiber for Everyone"
**Visual Theme:** "Real people, real connections, real value"
**Photography Style:** Authentic lifestyle, warm lighting, genuine emotions
**Color Palette:** AT&T Blue (#00A8E0), warm neutrals, natural accents

---

## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Photography Direction
- **Style:** Authentic lifestyle photography, not stock-looking
- **Lighting:** Natural window light with warm fill
- **Color Grading:** Warm tones (oranges/ambers) with AT&T blue accents
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, subject in environment, shallow depth of field
- **Mood:** Optimistic, relaxed, connected, stress-free

### Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Color:** AT&T Blue #00A8E0
- **Usage:** CTA buttons, accents, trust signals
- **Text:** Bold sans-serif (Helvetica/Arial bold family)
- **Logo Placement:** Bottom right or subtle background placement

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE PERSONAS

### Persona 1: "Budget-Conscious Brenda"
- **Demographics:** 30-45, household income $50k-$80k, homeowner
- **Pain Points:** Unexpected bills, contract lock-in, slow internet
- **Motivations:** Predictable costs, no contracts, reliable service
- **Best Offers:** $15/mo bundle, $35/mo no contract, 1st month free

### Persona 2: "Power User Paul"
- **Demographics:** 25-40, tech-savvy, gamer/creator
- **Pain Points:** Lag, slow uploads, buffering, unreliable connection
- **Motivations:** Speed, performance, gaming advantage
- **Best Offers:** 1000 Mbps $60/mo

### Persona 3: "Remote Worker Rachel"
- **Demographics:** 28-45, works from home, professional
- **Pain Points:** Dropped video calls, unreliable connection, unprofessional appearance
- **Motivations:** Professional video quality, stability, productivity
- **Best Offers:** 300 Mbps Zoom-focused, 1000 Mbps power user

### Persona 4: "Family-Focused Frank"
- **Demographics:** 35-50, family of 3-5, suburban home
- **Pain Points:** Kids complaining about slow internet, multiple devices fighting for bandwidth
- **Motivations:** Happy family, multiple streams, value
- **Best Offers:** All offers depending on usage level

## MESSAGING HIERARCHY

### Primary Headlines
- Benefit-focused ("No Buffering", "Work From Home Stability")
- Price-prominent ("$15/Month", "First Month FREE")
- Action-oriented ("Switch", "Upgrade", "Try")

### Supporting Copy
- Problem acknowledgment
- Solution presentation
- Proof point (speed/price)
- Soft CTA

### Disclaimers
- Legally required text
- Small but readable font
- Consistent placement (bottom)
